A site that finds Instagram heat maps for any location

Ever wanted to know what pictures are being taken around you? Or in some random town halfway across the country? We bring you on a scenic tour of the world - you choose your destinations. See the sights, experience the famous as well as the everyday, and even (beta users only) hear the secrets.


Special thanks to all those who helped us in getting off the ground, and to Whisper for their API.
Created at #HackAtBrown 1/24-5/2014 by Gareth, Jackie, Matt, Sara, Will, + Zach.
